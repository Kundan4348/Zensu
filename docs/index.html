禅数の遊び方

Objective — 目的

Win by landing any piece on your opponent's back row, or by capturing all opponent pieces.

Pieces — 駒

Each player has 12 pieces: 6 "Type I" (front) and 6 "Type II" (back). Numbers show exact movement distance in each direction.

Type I: ↑1 ←2 ↓3 →4
Type II: ↑2 ←3 ↓4 →1

Movement — 移動

  • Move exactly the number of spaces shown
  • Orthogonal only (no diagonals)
  • Can jump over your own pieces
  • Cannot move off the board

Capturing — 取る

  • Land on an opponent's piece to capture it
  • Jump over an opponent's piece to capture it
  • A single move can capture multiple pieces

先手 (Green) moves first.

勝

Green Wins!
